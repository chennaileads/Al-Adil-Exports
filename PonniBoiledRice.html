Ponni Boiled Rice – The Pride of Tamil Nadu

Al Adil Exports brings you the authentic Ponni Boiled Rice, a beloved staple from the heart of Tamil Nadu. Named after the Ponni (Cauvery) river that nourishes its cultivation, this medium-grain parboiled rice has been a trusted household name across South India for generations.

Ponni Boiled Rice undergoes a unique parboiling process where the paddy is partially boiled in the husk before milling. This traditional technique infuses nutrients from the bran into the grain, making it healthier, fluffier, and non-sticky when cooked. Its soft, easily digestible texture makes it ideal for everyday meals.

We supply premium-grade Ponni Boiled Rice sourced from the fertile delta regions of Tamil Nadu, processed in state-of-the-art mills, and packed with utmost care to preserve freshness and quality. Available for bulk domestic supply and international export.

Distinctive Features

  • Medium-grain rice with soft, fluffy texture after cooking
  • Parboiled in husk to retain natural nutrients and vitamins
  • Non-sticky grains that remain separate when cooked
  • Easy to digest – ideal for all age groups
  • Mild flavour that complements all South Indian dishes
  • Excellent for sambar rice, rasam rice, curd rice, and biryani
  • Naturally enriched with bran nutrients during parboiling

Product Specifications

  • Grain Type: Medium grain (5.0 mm – 5.5 mm)
  • Moisture Content: 12% – 14% max
  • Broken Grains: 2% – 5% (as per grade)
  • Purity: 95% – 98% (sortex cleaned)
  • Foreign Matter: Less than 1%
  • Parboiling Process: Steam-parboiled in husk
  • Origin: Cauvery Delta, Tamil Nadu, India
