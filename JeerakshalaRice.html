Jeerakshala Rice – Traditional Kerala Aromatic Rice

Al Adil Exports brings you the authentic Jeerakshala Rice, also known as Jeerakasala or Jeera Rice, a traditional aromatic short-grain variety cultivated in the fertile paddy fields of Kerala and Karnataka. This unique rice gets its name from its resemblance to cumin seeds (jeera) and carries a distinct natural fragrance that enhances any dish.

Jeerakshala Rice is a staple in Malabar cuisine, particularly famous for preparing the traditional Kerala-style biryani, ghee rice, neychoru, and coconut rice. Its small, slender grains cook to a soft yet non-sticky texture, absorbing flavours beautifully while maintaining their individuality.

We supply premium quality Jeerakshala Rice sourced directly from trusted farmers, processed with care, and packed to preserve its natural aroma and freshness. Available in bulk quantities for domestic and international markets.
